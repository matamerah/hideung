== Description ==

Hideung is made based on _s(https://github.com/Automattic/_s) theme by Automattic, combinated with Normalize.css and Skeleton-inspired media query. Also with simple and minimalist concept. Perfect for personal blog or even photographers to showcase their portfolio. Hideung mean is Black on Sundanese (Indonesia).

Clean blogging theme, good for you who wants focused on the content. It supports 1 widget areas on the sidebar. Also translation ready.

More features going to added in next version.

== Features ==

* Custom menus support.
* Background support.
* Header support.
* Child-theme Friendly.
* Focusing on the content.
* Translation-ready (contains .pot files).
